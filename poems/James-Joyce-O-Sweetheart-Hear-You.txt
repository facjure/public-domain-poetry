---
author: James Joyce 
title: O Sweetheart, Hear You
---

O Sweetheart, hear you
Your loverï¿½s tale;
A man shall have sorrow
When friends him fail.

For he shall know then
Friends be untrue
And a little ashes
Their words come to.

But one unto him
Will softly move
And softly woo him
In ways of love.

His hand is under
Her smooth round breast;
So he who has sorrow
Shall have rest.
